## Choosing a Provider

I had switched to a US-model iPhone XS. One major difference from the mainland China model is that the US model includes eSIM support, so testing that capability had been on my to-do list for some time.

I finally decided to try it. [eSIMDB](https://esimdb.com/china) lists eSIM plans for many regions around the world, including China.

![](/wp-content/uploads/2021/06/eSIM-for-China-%F0%9F%87%A8%F0%9F%87%B3-Find-the-best-data-plans-for-travelers-esimdb-2021-06-03-01-03-29-1024x747.png "eSIM plans for China, sorted by price")

When buying a plan, pay attention to its coverage area. Some plans only provide service in the Pearl River Delta.

After comparing several providers, I chose [3 Hong Kong](https://www.three.com.hk/), which appeared first when sorting by price. It was not actually the cheapest: the plan cost HKD 10 per day and included 500 MB of data before throttling. The minimum initial purchase was HKD 50, or roughly CNY 40, so I bought five days of service. The days could be activated separately at any time within the six-month validity period.

## Purchase

The purchase process was convenient and even supported Alipay. There were separate options for mainland Alipay and AlipayHK, which apparently operate as distinct systems.

![](/wp-content/uploads/2021/06/Three.com_.hk-DIY-Prepaid-2021-06-03-01-20-40-1024x570.png "Plan selection page")

Either the Greater China or Asia-Pacific plan would work for me, so I placed the order.

An activation email arrived after the purchase. This is one of the main advantages of an eSIM: a physical SIM would have to be shipped, and a provider might be reluctant to ship to China or the delivery might take too long.

![](/wp-content/uploads/2021/06/%E6%94%B6%E4%BB%B6%E7%AE%B1-%E2%80%A2-zcqzcq-2021-06-03-01-27-27.png "Similar to buying a local travel SIM before going abroad")

The remaining allowance can be checked on the provider's website.

![](/wp-content/uploads/2021/06/Three.com_.hk-DIY-Prepaid-2021-06-03-01-23-56.png "The data allowance was consumed surprisingly quickly")
